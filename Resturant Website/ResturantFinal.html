About Us

We bring traditional Hyderabadi flavors with a modern touch — fresh ingredients, expert chefs, and recipes that have simmered in our family kitchen for three generations. Every dish on this menu carries a story, from the first dum biryani we ever served to the haleem that now draws people across the city.
